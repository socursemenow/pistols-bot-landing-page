What is it

Pistols at Dawn brings the on-chain duelling game to Discord. Same mechanics, same depth; no contracts, no gas, no wallets.

⚔️

Classic Duel

Both duelists secretly pick four cards: when to fire, when to dodge, a tactic, and a blade. Cards lock, the duel resolves step by step. Three HP. One round. Someone falls.

🃏

34-Card Environment Deck

Every duel shuffles a fresh deck. One card drawn per step; damage up, hit chance down, or the rare All Shots Miss that ends careers. The deck shapes the duel more than the duelists do.

🗡️

Blades Phase

Survive the pistols and the blades come out. Rock-paper-scissors with stat modifiers baked in. Seppuku gives the strongest pre-shot buff in the game; at the cost of your life if both survive.

How a duel works

Four decisions. All chosen blind. All revealed at once.

Challenge

Type /duel @opponent and pick your premise. Honour, blood, or nothing at all.

Pick your cards

Fire step, dodge step, tactic, and blade. Private selection; nobody sees your hand.

Watch the duel

ASCII animation plays step by step. Environment cards shift the odds. Bullets fly. Someone falls.

Result

Winner declared; full combat log available. Rematch button ready. The queue takes the next one.

The cards

Every duelist picks four cards before the duel begins. No take-backs. No peeking. The environment does the rest.
